Who We Are

We pride ourselves in offering a fully comprehensive service from concept to planning and completion from site survey to a bespoke finished installation both in commercial and domestic environments. Our fitters are trained to guide and recommend on the correct materials and procedure to ensure a quality end product that will stand the test of time.

From moisture testing screeds, laitance removal, self-leveling, crack mat installation, underfloor heating, and over-boarding onto timber floors are all preparation procedures that are considered. Whether it be porcelain, composite, or natural stone products, we have the latest cutting equipment to deal with the most challenging materials, including large format sizes.
